Common Questions

What exactly do you monitor?

We run Google PageSpeed Insights on your site every week, tracking performance scores for both mobile and desktop. We also monitor Core Web Vitals like LCP, FID, and CLS — the metrics that actually matter for user experience.

Do I need to install anything?

Nope. We check your site from the outside, just like your visitors experience it. No plugins, no code changes, no tracking scripts. Just tell us your URL and we handle the rest.

How will you contact me?

Email only. We don't need your phone number or Slack workspace. If something needs your attention, you'll get a clear, actionable email. No dashboards to log into.

What if my score drops?

We'll let you know right away with specific recommendations. Sometimes it's a plugin update, sometimes it's image optimization, sometimes it's something else. We'll tell you what we found and what to do about it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If Anchor isn't working for you, just let us know and we'll part ways as friends.

What's the difference between Basic and Pro?

Basic gives you the essentials: weekly monitoring and alerts when something's wrong. Pro adds priority support, detailed monthly reports, and deeper performance insights. Most small business sites are perfectly fine with Basic.

Questions Before You Start?

Just reply to any email or reach out directly. We're real people who actually respond.
