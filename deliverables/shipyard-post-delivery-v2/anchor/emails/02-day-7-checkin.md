# Email 2: Day 7 Check-in

**When to send:** 7 days after launch
**CTA Type:** Soft CTA (relationship-building, no hard sell)
**Purpose:** Maintain relationship, reinforce "we don't disappear," gather feedback

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Your site's first week

---

**Body:**

Hi {{NAME}},

It's been a week since we launched {{URL}}. We're still here.

How's it going? Any feedback from customers? Anything feel off?

**Quick tips for week 2:**

- Add your site link to your email signature
- Share it on your social profiles if you haven't yet
- Ask 3 people you trust for honest feedback

If something needs adjusting, just reply to this email. We read everything and we're not going anywhere.

Here if you need us.

— Shipyard

P.S. Want ongoing support? Anchor Basic is $79/month. {{MAINTENANCE_LINK}}

## Voice Notes

This email is about relationship maintenance, not sales.

The goal is for the client to think: "Oh nice, they're checking in." Not: "Ugh, another sales email."

We use a different "we don't disappear" variation than Launch Day:
- Launch Day: "We're not disappearing."
- Day 7: "We're still here."

The P.S. mentions maintenance but doesn't push. Soft CTA strategy.

---

## What Success Looks Like

A client who receives this email should:
1. Feel cared for, not sold to
2. Reply with any issues they've noticed
3. Not hit "unsubscribe"
4. Remember Shipyard exists (brand reinforcement)
