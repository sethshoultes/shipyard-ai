How It Works

  1. Paste the script tag — SPARK automatically initializes when your page loads
  2. AI reads your page — Extracts content from your HTML (title, main text, meta tags)
  3. Visitors ask questions — Click the widget, type anything about your page
  4. Get instant answers — Claude responds in 1-2 seconds with accurate info
