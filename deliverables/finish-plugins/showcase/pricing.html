Pricing Questions

Everything you need to know about Wardrobe's pricing.

Why premium themes?

Premium themes fund our design and engineering work. Free themes will always exist and get regular updates. Premium themes are for users who want exclusive designs and priority support.

Will free themes stay free?

Yes, absolutely. Our five current themes (Ember, Forge, Slate, Drift, Bloom) are free and will always be free. Premium themes are in addition to the free tier, not instead of it.

What's included in premium?

Premium includes exclusive theme designs not available in free tier, priority email support, early access to new themes, and advanced customization options. Full details will be available when premium launches.

When does premium launch?

Premium is scheduled for Q3 2026. Sign up above to get notified as soon as it's available.

Can I use free themes forever?

Yes. Free themes are your forever option. Premium is optional for those who want exclusive designs and extra support.

Do I need a premium account to use Wardrobe?

No. All themes install via the same command: `npx wardrobe install [theme]`. Free themes are always available. Premium is opt-in.
