# Promptfolio

Promptfolio transforms your Claude conversation exports into beautiful, gallery-grade portfolio websites. Drop a Claude JSON export file into the upload zone and get a shareable URL in under 30 seconds. No accounts, no configuration, no friction.

The generator parses your conversation history, extracts prompts and responses, and renders them in a single sacred dark gallery template. Each portfolio gets a pre-generated Open Graph image for stunning social sharing. Everything is static HTML, CSS, and images served from a CDN.

Built for AI practitioners who want to showcase their work with pride. No analytics dashboards, no theme pickers, no forced attribution. Just your prompts, presented like art on a midnight gallery wall.
