Your AI assistant for local business. Auto-detects your business info, pre-populates FAQs, and adds a chat widget to your site.

== Description ==

Sous is your AI assistant — a warm, knowledgeable helper that answers customer questions right on your website.

**What it does:**
- Automatically detects your business name, category, hours, and contact info from your existing website content
- Pre-populates a set of smart FAQs tailored to your industry
- Adds a friendly chat widget to your site so visitors get instant answers
- Sends you a weekly digest with insights about what your customers are asking

**First weekly digest arrives Monday.**

**Built for:** Restaurants, dental practices, retail shops, and service businesses.

**Your AI assistant** handles the repetitive questions so you can focus on running your business.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to `/wp-content/plugins/sous/`, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" screen in WordPress.
3. Visit the Sous menu item in your admin dashboard to review your detected business info and launch your widget.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this work with any theme? =

Yes. Sous uses scoped CSS and vanilla JavaScript with no external dependencies.

= Where do the AI responses come from? =

Responses are generated by the Sous Cloudflare Worker endpoint using your business-specific FAQ templates.

= Is my data shared with third parties? =

No. Business detection runs locally on your server using your own site content.
