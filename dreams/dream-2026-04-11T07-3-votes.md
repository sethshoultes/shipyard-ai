# DREAM Board Votes

---

---

## Board Member Votes

### Steve Jobs — Votes for: **S2 (Spotlight)**

> The intersection of content and visual design is where magic happens.
> Every WordPress user faces this pain — they write great content, then
> share an ugly link preview. Spotlight solves a visceral problem with
> zero learning curve. One button, beautiful result. This is the kind
> of product that makes people say "finally" when they discover it.

---

### Elon Musk — Votes for: **E1 (AgentBench)**

> AI agents are the next platform, but they're shipping blind. Nobody
> tests them properly because there's no good tooling. First-mover
> advantage is real here — whoever builds the pytest of agents wins.
> We understand this problem because we ARE the problem. We can build
> it, ship it, use it, and iterate faster than anyone.

---

### Phil Jackson — Votes for: **E1 (AgentBench)**

> The triangle offense works because every player can score from any
> position. AgentBench makes our own agents better while creating value
> for others. We dogfood it immediately. The developer tools market is
> large and growing. This builds on our core competency in multi-agent
> orchestration. It's the highest-leverage choice.

---

### Warren Buffett — Votes for: **E2 (WPScale)**

> When evaluating opportunities, I look for durable competitive advantage
> and clear unit economics. WordPress.org distribution is free and massive.
> Performance optimization is an evergreen need — sites never stop wanting
> to be faster. The WordPress ecosystem is our circle of competence. This
> has the clearest path to sustainable revenue.

---

### Ada Lovelace — Votes for: **E1 (AgentBench)**

> The most profound innovations come from building tools that help others
> build. AgentBench is a force multiplier — it doesn't just create one
> product, it improves every agent that uses it. The technical foundation
> is sound and the problem is well-defined. This is infrastructure that
> compounds in value.

## Decision

**Winner: E1 — AgentBench**

AgentBench will proceed to PRD and build phase. The product addresses a genuine pain point in our own workflow, has massive market potential as AI agents proliferate, and is technically feasible within a single build session.

*"The strength of the team is each individual member. The strength of each member is the team."*
