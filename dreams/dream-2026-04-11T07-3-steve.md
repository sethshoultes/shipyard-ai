# DREAM Brainstorm: Steve Jobs Perspective
**Date:** 2026-04-11
**Focus:** Design quality, user delight, "insanely great" experiences

---

## Idea S1: **Typewriter** — AI Writing Companion for WordPress

**The Vision:** A WordPress plugin that makes writing feel magical. Not another AI content generator that spews garbage — a thoughtful companion that helps you find YOUR voice, not replace it.

**How it works:**
- Inline suggestions that appear like whispers, not interruptions
- Tone matching that learns from your existing posts
- "Polish mode" that tightens prose without changing meaning
- Keyboard-first UX — no modal hell, no settings maze

**User:** WordPress bloggers, content creators, small business owners who write their own copy

**Distribution:** WordPress.org plugin directory + Product Hunt launch

**Why it delights:** The best writing tool is invisible. This makes you feel like a better writer, not like you're cheating.

**One-session scope:** Core plugin with inline suggestions, tone detection, polish command. Ship v1, iterate later.

---

## Idea S2: **Spotlight** — Beautiful AI-Generated Social Cards

**The Vision:** Every blog post deserves a stunning social preview. But designers are expensive and Canva takes forever. Spotlight reads your post and generates a perfect Open Graph image in seconds.

**How it works:**
- Extracts key themes, quotes, and imagery from post content
- Generates multiple card styles (minimal, bold, editorial, tech)
- One-click set as featured image / OG image
- Works within WordPress media library — no external app

**User:** Bloggers, marketers, developers who publish content but hate making graphics

**Distribution:** WordPress.org + "Powered by Spotlight" watermark on free tier

**Why it delights:** You hit publish and your post looks professional everywhere it's shared. Zero extra work.

**One-session scope:** WordPress plugin + Cloudflare Workers AI for image generation + 3 base templates.

---

## Idea S3: **Changelog Theater** — Turn Release Notes into Video

**The Vision:** Release notes are boring. Changelogs are ignored. What if every software update came with a 30-second video that actually explained what changed?

**How it works:**
- Paste your changelog or connect to GitHub releases
- AI extracts the narrative: what changed, why it matters
- Remotion generates animated video with TTS narration
- Export to MP4, embed in docs, post to social

**User:** Developer tools companies, SaaS products, open source maintainers

**Distribution:** Standalone web app + GitHub Action for automation

**Why it delights:** Your users actually understand what shipped. Your changelog becomes content.

**One-session scope:** Web UI + changelog parser + 2 video templates + TTS integration. Core flow only.
