## Board Votes

### Phil Jackson (CEO/Coach) votes: **WP-Agent**

> The triangle offense works because every player knows their role.
> WP-Agent plays to ALL our strengths simultaneously — WordPress plugins,
> AI integration, multi-agent orchestration. It's the synthesis.
> Distribution is solved (WordPress.org). User is clear (business owners).
> And critically: we can ship a working MVP in one session.

---

### Steve Jobs (Design) votes: **ClipCraft**

> ClipCraft is the only idea here that creates *magic moments*.
> When someone clicks a button and watches their blog post
> transform into a polished video — that's the kind of delight
> that builds word-of-mouth. The technical foundation (Remotion + TTS)
> is already proven. This ships beautiful, not just functional.

---

### Elon Musk (Scale) votes: **WP-Agent**

> 43% of the web runs WordPress. If we nail the agent-per-site model,
> the TAM is effectively infinite. WP-Agent is also the gateway drug —
> once sites have AI agents, they'll want AgentForge to orchestrate them.
> This is the wedge product. Ship fast, learn faster, expand the moat.
> LocalGenius Lite is too narrow. This is the 10x play.

---

### Warren Buffett (Business Model) votes: **WP-Agent**

> I look for simple businesses I can understand with durable advantages.
> WP-Agent has the clearest path to recurring revenue: freemium plugin
> with usage-based pricing on AI queries. The moat is data — each site's
> agent gets smarter over time. And we're not dependent on any single
> customer. Diversified, scalable, and we eat our own cooking.

---

### Oprah Winfrey (User Empathy) votes: **WP-Agent**

> Every small business owner I know feels left behind by AI.
> They hear about ChatGPT but don't know how to use it for their
> plumbing company or yoga studio. WP-Agent meets them WHERE THEY ARE.
> Their website becomes intelligent without them learning anything new.
> This is democratizing AI in the truest sense.
