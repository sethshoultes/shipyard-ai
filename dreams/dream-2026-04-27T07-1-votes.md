# Board Votes
## Dream Cycle: 2026-04-27T07-1
---

## Steve's Pick: Loominate
**Justification:** Loominate is the most defensible expression of who we are. No one else on Earth has shipped both WordPress plugins and Remotion video pipelines. That intersection is our moat. The moment a writer sees their draft turn into a movie trailer, they are emotionally hooked. Distribution is organic — every video has a watermark linking back to the plugin. It is beautiful, it is ours, and it ships in one sitting.

---

## Elon's Pick: WP Sentinel
**Justification:** WP Sentinel combines the largest addressable market (43% of the web) with our most proven capability (one-session WP plugins). The technical stack is solved: PHP for the plugin shell, React for the chat UI, Cloudflare Worker for Claude proxy. The freemium model creates a perpetual acquisition funnel through wordpress.org. Support is a painkiller, not a vitamin. The unit economics are obvious and the build is low-risk.

---

## Phil's Pick: WP Sentinel
**Justification:** We win when we play team basketball — combining multiple strengths into one unstoppable play. WP Sentinel fuses our WP plugin speed, our AI integration chops, and our multi-agent orchestration into a product with a built-in distribution channel. We can dogfood it on every client site. The plugin ships in one session, the Worker in another, and the business model is immediate. This is the triangle offense: every strength touches the ball.

---

## Naval's Pick: WP Sentinel
**Justification:** Distribution beats product, but product-market fit with built-in distribution is unbeatable. WordPress.org is a search engine with 60,000 plugins and a billion active checks for updates. A freemium plugin there is a self-sustaining acquisition loop. Every install is a lead. Every health scan is a reason to upgrade. WP Sentinel has network effects through review velocity, compatibility data, and word-of-mouth among hosting providers. This is a compounding asset.

---

## Jensen's Pick: Agent Foundry
**Justification:** The compute graph for AI is shifting from single prompts to distributed agent topologies. Someone needs to build the observability and deployment layer for that topology. Agent Foundry is infrastructure. Infrastructure has higher margins, stickier contracts, and clearer enterprise value. We already have the Worker orchestration patterns; we just need a dashboard. This is where the industry is heading, and we should plant the flag before the Vercels of the world notice.
