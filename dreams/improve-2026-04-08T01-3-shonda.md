## The Retention Lens

*"The only thing that separates you from the people you admire is that you haven't started yet."*

Every product needs its "Sunday night" — the moment users can't wait to return. Grey's Anatomy works because you need to know what happens next. What's the cliffhanger for each product?

## LocalGenius

### Current Retention Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Strength | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Weekly digest email | Medium | Passive consumption, easy to ignore |
| Review notifications | Strong | Real urgency — reviews need responses |
| Social calendar | Medium | Only matters if using social features |
| Subscription payment | Weak | Billing reminder, not engagement driver |

### The Story Problem

LocalGenius helps businesses but doesn't create a narrative. Users complete tasks but don't progress through chapters. There's no "what happens next?" anticipation.

### Retention Opportunities

**1. The Competitive Storyline**
- Show ranking vs similar businesses in the area
- "This week: You moved from #8 to #5 in 'Austin Mexican Restaurants' reviews"
- Weekly leaderboard creates ongoing competition
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — gamification with real stakes

**2. Customer Journey Tracking**
- "Maria left a 4-star review in January. She visited again last week!"
- Show return customer stories
- Celebrate customer wins, not just metrics
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — emotional connection to outcomes

**3. "Your Marketing Calendar" View**
- Visual calendar showing past/scheduled content
- Empty days feel wrong — "You haven't posted in 5 days"
- Streaks for consistent activity
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — habit formation

**4. Monthly "Season Recap"**
- End-of-month summary email styled like a TV episode recap
- "Previously on Your Business: 12 reviews, 3.2K social impressions..."
- Preview of next month: "Coming up: Valentine's Day campaign ideas"
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — anticipation builder

**What's the Cliffhanger?**
> "You're 2 reviews away from 100 total. When you hit it, we'll trigger a celebration post!"

## Dash (WP Command Palette)

### Current Retention Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Strength | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Keyboard habit | Medium | Once learned, becomes reflexive |
| Time savings | Weak | Invisible benefit, easy to take for granted |
| Recent items | Weak | Convenience, not engagement |

### The Story Problem

Dash is a utility — invisible when working well. There's no ongoing narrative. Users don't think "I can't wait to use Dash tomorrow."

### Retention Opportunities

**1. "Your Dashboard" Stats**
- Weekly summary: "You saved ~23 minutes this week using Dash"
- Commands used, searches made, time saved
- Compare to last week: "Up 15% from last week!"
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — makes invisible value visible

**2. Command Discovery Journey**
- Progressively reveal advanced features
- "Pro tip: Try typing > for commands!"
- Achievement unlocks: "You've discovered 5 of 12 features"
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — gamification light

**3. Team Shortcuts (Pro Feature)**
- See what commands teammates use most
- Shared custom shortcuts library
- "Sarah created a shortcut: 'orders' → WooCommerce Orders"
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — social/team dynamics

**4. Plugin Update Alerts**
- "3 plugins have updates. Search 'updates' to manage them."
- Dash becomes the alert center for WP admin
- Daily reason to open Cmd+K
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — creates new use case

**What's the Cliffhanger?**
> "You've used Dash for 30 days straight! Unlock the 'Power User' badge tomorrow."

## Pinned (WP Sticky Notes)

### Current Retention Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Strength | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| @mention notifications | Strong | Direct personal relevance |
| Acknowledgment system | Medium | Social proof, accountability |
| Expiry/aging | Medium | Creates urgency but also deletion |

### The Story Problem

Pinned is episodic — notes appear and disappear. There's no continuous thread connecting sessions. Good for tasks, but no narrative arc.

### Retention Opportunities

**1. Team Activity Feed**
- "While you were away: 3 new notes, Sarah acknowledged 2"
- Catch-up mode when returning to dashboard
- Creates FOMO dynamic
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — social re-engagement

**2. Note Threads (v1.1)**
- Reply to notes to create conversations
- "RE: Website updates — Done! See the staging site."
- Notes become discussions, not just announcements
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — transforms from posting to conversing

**3. Weekly Team Digest**
- "This week on your board: 12 notes created, 8 resolved"
- Top contributors, most acknowledged notes
- Sent to all team members
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — external reminder to return

**4. Recurring Notes**
- "Every Monday: Review weekly analytics"
- Template notes that regenerate
- Standing rituals create habitual returns
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — builds routine

**What's the Cliffhanger?**
> "You have 2 unacknowledged notes waiting for your response."

- Users feel like they're training a team
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — relationship building

> "Your next dream cycle runs in 3 hours. Based on LocalGenius success, Great Minds is exploring franchise management tools."

## Shipyard AI

### Current Retention Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Strength | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Project delivery | Strong | During project, highly engaged |
| None post-delivery | ❌ | No reason to return after ship |

### The Story Problem

Shipyard is a one-night stand, not a relationship. Project ends, customer leaves. No sequel, no spinoff, no cliffhanger.

### Retention Opportunities

**1. Site Health Dashboard (CRITICAL)**
- Post-launch: "Your site had 1.2K visitors this week"
- Lighthouse scores, uptime, performance metrics
- "Suggestion: Add a blog to improve SEO — want us to build it?"
- **Retention Impact:** CRITICAL — creates ongoing relationship

**2. Quarterly Site Refresh Proposals**
- "Q2 trends in your industry: minimalist hero sections"
- "We could update your homepage to match — 100K tokens, $X"
- Proactive suggestions keep the conversation going
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — upsell with value

**3. Portfolio Showcase Incentive**
- "Be featured in our portfolio — get 10% off your next project"
- Testimonial request → discount cycle
- Creates reciprocity and re-engagement
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — transactional but effective

**4. "How Your Site is Performing" Email Series**
- Week 1: "Your site is live! Here's day 1 traffic"
- Week 4: "First month report — 500 visitors, 23 contact form submissions"
- Week 12: "Quarter 1 done — time for a content refresh?"
- **Retention Impact:** HIGH — narrative arc post-delivery

**5. Annual Anniversary**
- "Happy 1 year! Your site has served 15K visitors"
- Offer: "Anniversary refresh — 20% off a redesign"
- Celebrates the relationship
- **Retention Impact:** MEDIUM — emotional connection

**What's the Cliffhanger?**
> "Your site traffic increased 40% last month. Want to see why — and how to keep the momentum?"

## The Universal Retention Framework

### Why People Return to Products

1. **Progress** — Am I moving forward toward a goal?
2. **Status** — How do I compare to others/my past self?
3. **Anticipation** — What happens next?
4. **Social** — Who's waiting for me?
5. **Fear of Loss** — What will I miss if I don't return?

### Product-by-Product Gap Analysis

| Product | Progress | Status | Anticipation | Social | Fear of Loss |
|---------|----------|--------|--------------|--------|--------------|
| LocalGenius | ⚠️ Weak | ❌ None | ⚠️ Weak | ❌ None | ✅ Reviews |
| Dash | ❌ None | ❌ None | ❌ None | ❌ None | ❌ None |
| Pinned | ⚠️ Weak | ⚠️ Weak | ⚠️ Weak | ✅ Strong | ⚠️ Weak |
| Great Minds | ✅ Strong | ❌ None | ✅ Strong | ❌ None | ⚠️ Weak |
| Shipyard | ❌ None | ❌ None | ❌ None | ❌ None | ❌ None |

**Biggest Gaps:** Status and Social are missing almost everywhere.

## Top 3 Retention Priorities

### 1. Shipyard: Post-Delivery Engagement System (CRITICAL)

**Why:** Currently 0% retention after project completion. Massive opportunity.

**The Arc:**
- **Episode 1:** Launch celebration + first week stats
- **Episode 2:** Monthly performance report
- **Episode 3:** Quarterly refresh suggestion
- **Episode 4:** Anniversary celebration + upsell
- **Ongoing:** Site health dashboard always available

**Implementation:**
- Automated email sequence post-delivery
- Client dashboard showing site metrics
- Proactive outreach with update suggestions

**Retention Target:** 40% of clients engage with at least one post-launch touchpoint

### 2. LocalGenius: Competitive Storyline (HIGH)

**Why:** SMB owners are competitive. Ranking creates stakes.

**The Arc:**
- "You're #8 in Austin Mexican Restaurants"
- Weekly update on ranking movement
- Tips to improve: "Add 2 reviews to pass Casa Ole"
- Celebration when ranking improves

**Implementation:**
- Aggregate anonymized review data by category/location
- Simple ranking algorithm (reviews × recency × rating)
- Weekly email with ranking update

**Retention Target:** 25% increase in weekly active engagement

### 3. Pinned: Note Threads + Team Feed (HIGH)

**Why:** Notes are monologues; threads are conversations.

**The Arc:**
- Post note → Teammate replies → You reply → Resolution
- Feed shows all activity while you were away
- Unacknowledged notes create return obligation

**Implementation:**
- Add reply functionality to notes (v1.1)
- Activity feed in widget header
- Push unacknowledged count to email

**Retention Target:** 2x daily active users among teams
