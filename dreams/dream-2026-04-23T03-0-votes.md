## Board Votes

### Phil Jackson (CEO/Coach) votes: **AgentPipe**

> The triangle offense requires every player to read the floor and find the open man.
> AgentPipe is the open man — it connects our three strongest capabilities
> (WordPress plugins, AI integrations, agent orchestration) into a single play.
> Distribution is solved. Technical risk is low. And if MCP becomes the standard
> protocol for AI context, owning the WordPress bridge is a generational position.

---

### Steve Jobs (Design) votes: **TypeTide**

> The web is ugly by default. TypeTide is the only idea here that makes
> something fundamentally *better* by removing friction and adding taste.
> It ships beautiful by default, not as an afterthought. Every post becomes
> a reading experience worth remembering. That is the product I want to see
> in the world — and on our own site.

---

### Elon Musk (Engineering) votes: **AgentPipe**

> MCP is the protocol layer of the next five years of AI tooling.
> WordPress is 43% of the web. The intersection of those two curves
> is a massive TAM with near-zero competition today. We can ship the
> MVP in one session — it's REST endpoints with schema. The moat
> deepens as the ecosystem grows. PromptLocker is nice but crowded.
> AgentPipe is a wedge into the infrastructure layer.

---

### Warren Buffett (Business Model) votes: **AgentPipe**

> I look for businesses with durable competitive advantages and pricing power.
> AgentPipe starts as a free plugin, but the freemium conversion happens
> naturally — as AI queries scale, sites need higher limits and premium features.
> The switching cost is integration depth: once an agent workflow depends on
> your site's MCP endpoint, the site owner doesn't migrate lightly.
> Recurring revenue, low churn, high margins. That's a business.

---

### Oprah Winfrey (User Empathy) votes: **EchoFrame**

> I talk to content creators every day. They pour hours into writing something
> beautiful, then struggle to make it travel on social media. EchoFrame turns
> their words into voice, their voice into motion, and gives them dignity in
> a feed full of noise. It meets creators where they hurt. AgentPipe is
> powerful, but EchoFrame is *felt*.
