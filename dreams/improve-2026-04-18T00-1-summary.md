## Executive Summary

Four board members reviewed five shipped products. The verdict is unanimous: **LocalGenius is the portfolio's best opportunity for growth, and it's leaving money on the table across all dimensions — moat, conversion, revenue, and retention.**

Shipyard AI has strong technology but weak business fundamentals. The WordPress plugins (Dash, Pinned Notes) and Great Minds Plugin are solid but non-revenue.

## Cross-Board Themes

### Theme 1: LocalGenius Consensus
All four directors identified LocalGenius as the highest-leverage improvement opportunity:
- Jensen: Proprietary data capture (Business Journal)
- Oprah: Demo video to improve conversion
- Warren: Annual billing + upgrade path
- Shonda: Daily notifications + milestones

**This is the focus area.**

### Theme 2: Shipyard AI Business Model
Warren flagged the critical gap: Shipyard is a service business, not a SaaS business. No recurring revenue = no compounding value.

**Fix:** Maintenance plans and agency retainers.

### Theme 3: Show Don't Tell
Oprah identified that multiple products describe capabilities but don't demonstrate them. This is especially acute for Shipyard AI (what is Emdash?).

**Fix:** Add visual proof (video, GIF, portfolio) to homepages.

### Theme 4: Invisible Moats
Jensen noted that products generate valuable data but don't surface it to users. If users can't see the AI learning, they don't value it.

**Fix:** Add learning indicators and intelligence scores.

## Top 3 Improvements (Ranked by Cross-Board Impact)

### #1: LocalGenius Engagement System
**Impact: HIGH (affects moat, retention, revenue)**

| Dimension | Problem | Solution |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Moat (Jensen) | No proprietary data capture | Business Journal — owner annotations |
| Retention (Shonda) | Only weekly touchpoint | Daily micro-notifications + milestones |
| Revenue (Warren) | No expansion triggers | In-app upgrade prompts with locked features |
| Conversion (Oprah) | Abstract value prop | Weekly digest as demo content |

**Combined improvement:** A unified engagement system that:
1. Sends daily micro-notifications ("3 views today")
2. Prompts weekly annotations ("What drove your results?")
3. Celebrates milestones ("100th review!")
4. Shows trends with narrative ("Up 22% — best month yet")
5. Ends with cliffhangers ("I'm testing something new next week")
6. Surfaces upgrade opportunities at natural friction points

**Estimated impact:**
- +50% DAU/WAU ratio (daily engagement)
- +15% monthly retention
- +25% Base → Pro conversion
- Proprietary training data accumulation

**PRD Required: YES** — This is a significant feature set that warrants structured development.

## PRD Pipeline Recommendations

Based on this review, two PRDs should be created:

**Priority: P0**

### PRD 2: Shipyard AI Business Model Upgrade
**Priority: P1**
**Scope:**

**Why:** Converts one-time service revenue into recurring SaaS revenue. Required for investability.
