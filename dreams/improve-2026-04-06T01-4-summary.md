## Executive Summary

The board reviewed all five shipped products: LocalGenius, Shipyard AI, Dash, Pinned, and Great Minds Plugin. Across four distinct lenses—moat building, first-5-minutes experience, revenue opportunity, and retention—a clear consensus emerged:

**We have excellent products with weak business fundamentals.**

The technical execution is strong. The gaps are in monetization, onboarding clarity, and retention mechanics. Fixing these doesn't require new features—it requires making the value we've built visible and capturable.

## Top 3 Improvements Ranked by Impact

### #1: LocalGenius Revenue Activation + Morning Briefing
**Impact:** HIGH | **Effort:** MEDIUM | **Consensus:** 4/4 board members

**What:** Launch LocalGenius paid tier at $149/mo with a "Morning Briefing" daily notification that creates habitual engagement.

**Why This Wins:**
- Buffett: Highest revenue potential, clearest path to $1M ARR
- Shonda: Morning Briefing creates daily anticipation and habit
- Jensen: Usage data becomes moat-building intelligence
- Oprah: Pricing transparency increases trust

**Components:**
1. Pricing page with $149/mo SaaS model
2. Daily 8am "Morning Briefing" push notification
3. Usage analytics to track engagement and inform intelligence index

**Outcome:** Immediate revenue generation + retention flywheel + data accumulation

---

### #2: Shipyard Care Maintenance Tier + Site Performance Story
**Impact:** HIGH | **Effort:** MEDIUM | **Consensus:** 4/4 board members

**What:** Add post-launch maintenance plans ($99-$499/mo) with monthly "Site Performance Story" reports.

**Why This Wins:**
- Buffett: Transforms one-time revenue into recurring
- Shonda: Site Performance Story creates ongoing relationship
- Jensen: Performance benchmarks become proprietary data
- Oprah: Proves quality over time, builds trust for repeat business

**Components:**
1. Three-tier Care plans (Basic $99, Pro $249, Enterprise $499)
2. Automated monthly Site Performance Story emails
3. Performance benchmark dashboard comparing to all Shipyard sites

**Outcome:** 2-3x LTV increase + retention through value visibility + pattern library data

---

### #3: WordPress.org Distribution + Pro Tier for Dash
**Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH | **Effort:** LOW | **Consensus:** 4/4 board members

**What:** Publish Dash to WordPress.org with freemium model—free core + $59/year Pro tier with analytics and team features.

**Why This Wins:**
- Oprah: Eliminates installation friction, adds social proof via reviews
- Buffett: Creates scalable revenue (target 17K Pro customers = $1M ARR)
- Shonda: Weekly "Time Saved" report creates visible progress
- Jensen: Usage analytics enable AI-powered command suggestions (future moat)

**Components:**
1. WordPress.org submission and listing
2. Pro tier at $59/year with license management
3. Weekly "Time Saved" report feature
4. Usage analytics collection (with privacy controls)

**Outcome:** Distribution at scale + monetization + data for future AI features
