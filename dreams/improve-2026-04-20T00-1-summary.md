## Cross-Board Consensus

Three themes emerged from all four reviews:

### 1. LocalGenius is the Revenue Engine — Protect and Grow It
All four board members agree: LocalGenius is the portfolio's core business. But it has gaps:
- **Jensen:** Data moat underleveraged (no cross-customer benchmarks)
- **Oprah:** No demo showing the product in action
- **Buffett:** No annual billing, no referral program
- **Shonda:** No progress visualization in weekly digests

**Verdict:** LocalGenius improvements compound across all metrics — retention, revenue, moat, first-5-minutes.

### 2. Shipyard Needs Proof and Recurring Revenue
The 100% ship rate is a powerful claim, but unverifiable:
- **Jensen:** Zero visible portfolio = zero social proof
- **Oprah:** No PRD → shipped example to make transformation concrete
- **Buffett:** Transactional revenue model — no subscription layer
- **Shonda:** No ongoing relationship after ship

**Verdict:** Shipyard needs case studies (proof) and a maintenance subscription (revenue).

### 3. WordPress Plugins Are Correctly Positioned
All board members agree: Dash and Pinned are marketing assets, not products.
- **Jensen:** GREEN moat status for lead-gen tools
- **Buffett:** ROI is CAC reduction, not direct revenue
- Minor UX improvements (onboarding, welcome notes) are worth doing but not urgent

**Verdict:** Continue as lead-gen. Don't over-engineer.

## Top 3 Improvements Ranked by Impact

### #1: LocalGenius Annual Billing + Progress Visualization
**Impact: CRITICAL** | **Effort: LOW** | **Ship: 1 week**

**What:**
- Add annual billing option with 20% discount (~$278/year vs $348/year)
- Add "This Month vs. Last Month" comparison to weekly digest

**Why:**
- Buffett: Annual billing pulls forward 2.4 months of revenue (if 30% adopt) and reduces churn 40%
- Shonda: Progress visualization creates narrative arc — users see themselves winning

**Combined Effect:** Higher retention + higher lifetime value + stronger emotional connection. This is the single highest-ROI change across all reviews.

**Who:** LocalGenius engineering + product
**Depends on:** Nothing — can start immediately

---

### #2: Shipyard Case Study + Maintenance Subscription
**Impact: HIGH** | **Effort: MEDIUM** | **Ship: 2 weeks**

**What:**
- Publish 1 sanitized case study showing PRD → deployed product
- Launch "Shipyard Maintenance" subscription ($500/month for 2 revision rounds + priority queue)

**Why:**
- Jensen: Converts "100% ship rate" from claim to proof
- Oprah: Makes the PRD → production transformation concrete and believable
- Buffett: Creates recurring revenue from one-time transactions

**Combined Effect:** Addresses both trust gap (case study) and revenue model (subscription). This unlocks Shipyard's growth potential.

**Who:** Shipyard team (content + productization)
**Depends on:** Client permission for case study

---

### #3: LocalGenius Demo Video
**Impact: MEDIUM-HIGH** | **Effort: LOW** | **Ship: 3 days**

**What:**
- Record 60-second demo video showing: owner asks question → AI suggests marketing action → owner approves with one click
- Embed on homepage above the fold

**Why:**
- Oprah: Users can't visualize using the product — show, don't tell
- Jensen: Differentiation is hard to communicate in text — video makes it visceral

**Combined Effect:** Converts "interesting" visitors to trial signups. Low effort, high conversion impact.

**Who:** Marketing + product (screen recording)
**Depends on:** Access to LocalGenius dashboard with sample data
