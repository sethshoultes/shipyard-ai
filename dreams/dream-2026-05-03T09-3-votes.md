# DREAM Board Votes

**Date:** 2026-05-03
**Candidates:** 6 ideas (3 Steve, 3 Elon)
**Rule:** Each board member picks their #1 from all 6 ideas. 5-line justification maximum.

---

## Sarah — Design Lead

**Pick:** Zenith (Steve #1)

**Justification:** WordPress plugins are our fastest path to revenue. A block that feels native to Gutenberg, not bolted-on, creates instant trust. The AI image space is crowded but the WP-native UX isn't. One session, immediate dogfooding on our own blog. Design integrity wins when everything else looks like a dashboard thrown at a wall.

---

## Mike — CTO

**Pick:** AgentPress (Elon #1)

**Justification:** We've already built the orchestration layer. Packaging it as a WP plugin turns our internal tooling into a platform. The registry pattern means every new agent we build automatically makes the product stronger. WP's REST API makes the integration trivial, and the extensibility hook means developers do our marketing for us.

---

## Alex — Head of Product

**Pick:** AgentPress (Elon #1)

**Justification:** This is the only idea that creates a platform rather than a point solution. Other plugins become our distribution channel when they integrate. The freemium model is proven in WP. Technical risk is low because we're just exposing what already works. The others are features; AgentPress is an ecosystem.

---

## Jordan — Growth

**Pick:** AgentPress (Elon #1)

**Justification:** WP plugin directory is the best free acquisition channel in software. 60,000 plugins, but almost zero competition in the agent orchestration category. Every WP site is a potential node in our network. The compounding effect of other developers building on our registry is unmatched. Network effects beat linear sales.

---

## Taylor — CEO

**Pick:** AgentPress (Elon #1)

**Justification:** This combines every advantage we have: WP expertise, multi-agent orchestration, hybrid AI architecture, and SaaS mindset. The others are features or tools; AgentPress is a platform. In one session we can ship a router, three agents, and a registry that makes developers sticky. It is the single most defensible idea on the board.
