# Warren Buffett — Board Review: ClipCraft Backend

**Verdict: No business here. Not even a pretense of one.**

- **Unit economics:** Catastrophic. Every render burns ~$0.30-$0.50 in OpenAI + compute. No price tag on the output. Losing money on every user before coffee.
- **Revenue model:** Hobby. No pricing page. No subscription. No usage tiers. PRD describes a cost ceiling, not a revenue floor. A business without prices is a charity.
- **Competitive moat:** None. GPT-4 summary + OpenAI TTS + Remotion = off-the-shelf parts. Any teenager with a weekend and a credit card replicates this. No brand, no switching costs, no network effects.
- **Capital efficiency:** Abysmal. We spent engineering cycles to deliver a `layout.tsx`. Zero return on deployed capital. Cloudflare free tier won't cover inference bills. Need continuous capital infusion just to keep lights on.
- **Durable value:** Missing entirely. No retained earnings potential. No pricing power. No widening moat with scale. Costs rise linearly with users. Economics get worse, not better, as we grow.

**Score: 1/10.** Buying a lottery ticket has better expected return.
