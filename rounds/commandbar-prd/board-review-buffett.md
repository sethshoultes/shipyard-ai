# Board Review — CommandBar PRD
**Warren Buffett | Great Minds Agency Board**

---

## Verdict
Not a business. A neat toy with no toll bridge.

---

## Unit Economics
- Zero revenue. Free GPL plugin. No premium tier, no support fees, no SaaS upsell.
- CAC near-zero (organic WordPress.org traffic). Serving cost also near-zero (client-side JS).
- Unit economics: undefined. Division by zero.

## Revenue Model
- None. PRD explicitly states "free plugin."
- No monetization roadmap. Not even a "maybe we charge later" footnote.
- If this drives agency consulting leads, say so. Otherwise: hobby.

## Competitive Moat
- None. Two files, ~550 lines, zero proprietary tech.
- Competitor rebuilds this in one afternoon. Several already exist.
- `beam_items` filter hook is nice, not a moat. Trivial to replicate.
- No switching costs. Users uninstall in 10 seconds.
- No network effects. No data flywheel.

## Capital Efficiency
- Frugal build. One dev, one session, no infrastructure.
- But capital without return is expense, not investment.
- 4 hours of developer time × $0 price = bad math.
- If used as agency marketing, cheap billboard. If standalone product, wasted keystrokes.

## Suggested Hard Questions
1. Where is the pricing page?
2. What prevents Automattic from shipping this in core WordPress next quarter?
3. How do 500 free installs become $1 of profit?
4. Is this a product, or a blog post prop?

## Score
**3/10**

Well-executed vapor. No economic castle, no toll bridge, no durable value. Would not allocate a dime of Berkshire capital here. Recommend: attach a paid tier or kill it and bill the hours to a real client.
