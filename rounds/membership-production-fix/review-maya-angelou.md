# Maya Angelou Review: Membership Copy

## Verdict
Sterile. Corporate crutches everywhere.

## What's Wrong

**Rhythm**: None. Every error message same cadence. Same structure. Same breath.
- "We couldn't find..." (6 times)
- "Something went wrong on our end — please try again" (11 times)
- "Please [action]..." (8 times)

**Voice**: Sounds like legal wrote it. Too polite. Too apologetic. No personality.

**Trying too hard**:
- "That email doesn't look right — please check and try again" (hedging with em-dash)
- "Hold on — we're still processing your previous request" (fake casual)
- "Something went wrong on our end — please try again" (robotic apology loop)

**Missing**: Confidence. Warmth. A real person talking.

## The 3 Weakest Lines

### 1. "Something went wrong on our end — please try again"
**Why it fails**: Generic error 101. Says nothing. Helps no one.

**Rewrite**:
> "Hmm. Try that again?"

---

### 2. "We couldn't find a membership with that email"
**Why it fails**: Passive. Distant. Like reading a database error.

**Rewrite**:
> "No account found. Check your email?"

---

### 3. "That email doesn't look right — please check and try again"
**Why it fails**: Wordy. The em-dash pretends to be friendly but just adds syllables.

**Rewrite**:
> "Email looks off. Double-check it?"

---

## Pattern to Break
Stop saying "we." Start breathing. One person to another.
