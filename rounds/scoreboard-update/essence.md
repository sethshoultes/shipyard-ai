# Essence

**What it is:**
Monument to execution that doesn't apologize.

**The feeling:**
Undeniable credibility. Pride mixed with accountability.

**What must be perfect:**
Truth. Every line earned.

**Creative direction:**
Unflinching honesty, scannable depth.

---

**Core tension:**
Speed vs meaning. Both win. Compact summary table (32 rows, scan in seconds). Depth on recent work (context, not theater).

**Voice:**
PASS. BLOCK. REJECT. Raw verdicts. No marketing speak. Engineers talking to engineers.

**Non-negotiable:**
Show failures. Write "—" when data missing. Never hide to look good. Scoreboard is mirror, not brochure.

**Format:**
Two sections. Top: full table, every project visible. Bottom: top 5 get the story—deliverables, QA notes, board feedback.

**Ship criteria:**
Honest > complete > perfect. Manual v1 forces care. Automate when format proven.
