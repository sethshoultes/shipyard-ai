# Essence

A deploy gate that stops phantom routes before they become 404s.

## What it REALLY is
The final "go/no-go" before a Next.js static export ships an invisible corpse.

## Feeling
Friday 5pm confidence. Not hope. Certainty.

## The one thing that must be perfect
The failure message: one crisp sentence, zero ambiguity, zero noise.

## Creative direction
Truth before launch.

## Non-negotiables
- Binary output only: `Ready.` or one sentence explaining death.
- Zero config. Reads next.config.ts and filesystem. Nothing else.
- No warnings. Yellow is fear. We do not traffic in fear.
