# Steve Jobs — Chief Design & Brand Officer
## Position: Standalone Apps Portfolio

**Naming.** One word. Human. Memorable. "Scribe." "Cut." "Tuned." These sing. "AgentPipe" sounds like a plumbing supply catalog. "Promptfolio" is a portmanteau crime — it reeks of cheap WordPress plugins. "Whisper Blocks" has three syllables too many; if it turns voice into content, call it "Whisper." Period. A name is a promise. Make it whisper, not shout.

**Design philosophy.** This is not a warehouse. It is a gallery. Most agencies show you a veneer; we leave the garage door open. You see the sawdust, the half-built chair, the finished table. That honesty is the feature. Every gradient, every status badge, every browser chrome must say: *we ship.* If it does not inspire envy, burn it.

**First 30 seconds.** You land on `/work` and you feel velocity. The gradients shift. The status badges pulse with truth — green for shipped, amber for building, slate for raw scaffold. No fog. No marketing haze. In thirty seconds you know exactly what is real, what is cooking, and what is merely a spark. That clarity is intoxicating.

**Brand voice.** We speak like craftsmen, not SaaS marketers. Short sentences. No "leveraging AI to drive synergy." We say: *This cuts video. It works. Here is the code.* The product is the prose. If you need adjectives to sell it, the product is not good enough.

**What to say NO to.** NO to fabricated screenshots — a scaffold with a placeholder image is a lie. NO to search bars and filters — if you need search, you have already failed at curation. NO to 404 demo links. NO to "read more" walls of text. NO to duplicated data — one source of truth, or we do not ship. Simplicity is not the absence of clutter; it is the presence of discipline.

**Emotional hook.** People will love this because it is *naked.* In an industry drowning in polished case studies, we show the workshop. That transparency is intimacy. It builds trust faster than any deck ever could. When you see the honesty, you believe the craft.
