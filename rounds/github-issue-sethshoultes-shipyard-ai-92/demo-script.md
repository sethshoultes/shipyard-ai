# REEL — 2-Minute Demo Script

---

NARRATOR
Two thousand words. Market analysis. Best thing I've written all quarter.

[SCREEN: Blog post. Clean prose. Scroll bar tiny.]

NARRATOR
Views? Twelve. Dwell time? Twenty seconds. My mother read it twice. That counts.

[SCREEN: Analytics dashboard. Flatline.]

NARRATOR
Boss walks by. "Video." Client calls. "Vertical. Polished. Yesterday." I look at my hands. These hands do not know After Effects.

[SCREEN: After Effects timeline. Layers like spaghetti. Cursor panics.]

NARRATOR
I open Reel.

[SCREEN: Stark white page. One input field. Nothing else.]

NARRATOR
Paste the URL. Hit enter.

[SCREEN: URL drops in. Spinner spins.]

NARRATOR
Machine reads two thousand words. Finds the spine. Three beats. Four. Five. The sentences people quote at dinner parties. Not a summary. Extraction.

[SCREEN: Key points populate. Bold. Confident. Numbered.]

NARRATOR
Voice selector. Three options. Not forty. Not a catalog that needs its own search bar. Curated. Human. Middle one's already selected in case you're in a hurry.

[SCREEN: Three voice cards. Middle voice glows.]

NARRATOR
Big button. Red. "Make Reel." Click.

[SCREEN: Finger clicks. Button depresses.]

NARRATOR
No templates to browse. No fonts to fight over. No hex-code existentialism at two in the afternoon. Zero customization. Zero is freedom when you're bleeding on a deadline.

[SCREEN: Progress bar. Text below: "a few minutes."]

NARRATOR
Honest timing. Not "sixty seconds." Not "rendered before you blink." A few minutes. Long enough to answer Slack. Short enough to survive.

[SCREEN: Phone buzzes on desk. Notification: "Your Reel is ready."]

NARRATOR
Download link. MP4. Ten-eighty by nineteen-twenty. Vertical. One template. One font pair. Engineered to work.

[SCREEN: Phone held upright. Video plays.]

NARRATOR
Title card snaps in. Bullet one lands on the beat. Bullet two. Voice syncs perfectly. Outro resolves like a chord.

[SCREEN: Smooth motion. Clean typography. Polished. Professional.]

NARRATOR
My blog post. My voice. Cinema.

[SCREEN: Black. Logo: REEL.]

NARRATOR
One button. Then cinema.
