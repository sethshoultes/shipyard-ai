# Shonda Retention Roadmap — Scribe v1.1
## What Keeps Users Coming Back

> "Beautifully engineered utility with no soul and no reason to binge." — Board Review

The fix: give creators a stage, a mirror, and a reason to tune in next week.

---

## 1. The Library ("Your Body of Work")

**Problem:** Transcripts bury themselves inside individual WordPress posts. Users cannot see everything they have built.

**v1.1 Feature:** Transcript Library View
- Dedicated admin screen: `/wp-admin/scribe-library`
- Grid of all transcripts with thumbnail waveform, title, date, duration, and status
- Filter by month, by post, by audio source
- One-click "Open in Editor" and "View Public Page"
- **Hook:** Creators log in just to see the shelf fill up. Vanity is a hell of a drug.

---

## 2. The Dashboard ("Your Stats This Month")

**Problem:** No dashboard. No stats. No "your transcripts this month."

**v1.1 Feature:** Creator Dashboard Widget
- Minutes transcribed this month vs. cap (visual progress bar)
- Total transcripts all-time
- Most-played sentence / most-shared timestamp
- Streak counter: "🔥 3-week upload streak"
- **Hook:** Monthly caps feel like walls until you wrap them in a progress bar. Then they feel like a game.

---

## 3. Public Transcript Pages (The Shareable Stage)

**Problem:** Output is trapped in WordPress. No shareable URLs. No timestamp deep-links.

**v1.1 Feature:** Public Transcript URLs
- Auto-generated front-end page per transcript: `yoursite.com/transcript/slug`
- Click any sentence to copy timestamped link
- Clean typography, responsive, SEO-friendly
- Optional password protection or unlisted mode
- **Hook:** Readers share timestamps. Every share is a referral. Every referral is a reason to come back and upload more.

---

## 4. Embeddable Player Block (Audience Everywhere)

**Problem:** Podcasters build audiences — this tool gives them zero help doing so.

**v1.1 Feature:** Embeddable Transcript Player
- `<iframe>` or oEmbed code for each transcript
- Play audio + highlight current sentence in sync
- Minimal branding, customizable accent color
- **Hook:** Creators embed on Substack, Ghost, or client sites. Their audience sees the tool. Their audience wants the tool.

---

## 5. Template Gallery (Not Just Typography — *Theirs*)

**Problem:** "Gorgeous typography" is one-size-fits-all. No template gallery.

**v1.1 Feature:** Template System
- Pre-built templates: Podcast Interview, Lecture Notes, Journalism Q&A, Minimalist
- Users save custom templates as defaults
- Template marketplace (future: community submissions)
- **Hook:** Users return to tweak, collect, and show off their house style.

---

## 6. Weekly Email Report (The Cliffhanger)

**Problem:** Plugin goes silent after upload. No mystery. No next episode.

**v1.1 Feature:** Creator Weekly Digest
- Subject: "Your transcript got 147 plays this week"
- Opens, plays, top timestamp, shares
- New feature teasers: "Word-level precision now available — see how it looks on your latest episode"
- **Hook:** Vanity metrics are a scheduled reason to reopen the plugin.

---

## 7. Emotional Async Hooks (The Waiting Room)

**Problem:** Async processing is suspense, but the spec demands it feel "invisible." Wrong choice.

**v1.1 Feature:** Processing Theater
- Status copy that teases craft: "Detecting speakers…", "Choosing typography…", "Polishing timestamps…"
- Optional micro-preview: reveal first 3 sentences as they finish
- Estimated time remaining with personality ("About the length of a coffee break")
- **Hook:** Waiting becomes part of the show. Anticipation is retention.

---

## 8. Precision Unlocks (Upgrade Tension)

**Problem:** No unlocks. No "Word-level precision available — upgrade to see."

**v1.1 Feature:** Gated Precision Tiers
- Free: paragraph-level timestamps
- Paid: word-level precision, speaker diarization, custom vocabulary
- Inline upsell: "Speaker labels detected — unlock to reveal"
- **Hook:** The transcript itself becomes a trailer for the paid tier.

---

## 9. Content Flywheel Metrics (Internal)

**Problem:** No data network effect. Users leave,带走 nothing but CSS.

**v1.1 Instrumentation:**
- Share-to-upload ratio (how many public views drive new accounts)
- Template reuse rate
- Email open → re-activation rate
- Streak correlation with retention
