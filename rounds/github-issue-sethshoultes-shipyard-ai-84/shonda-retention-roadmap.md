# Shonda Retention Roadmap — Poster Child v1.1

## What Keeps Users Coming Back

A one-night stand is not a series. To turn a single PNG export into a habit, the product needs recurring characters, cliffhangers, and a reason to hit "next episode."

### 1. "Your Posters" — The Gallery
- Every generated poster is saved to a user-scoped gallery (anonymous session or opted-in account).
- Users can browse, compare, and delete past renders.
- Thumbnails load instantly; clicking enters a detail view with share stats.

### 2. The Star Threshold Auto-Refresh
- When a repo crosses a star milestone (100, 500, 1k, 10k), the poster auto-refreshes.
- Notify the user: "Your repo just hit 1,000 stars. Here's your updated poster."
- Creates a recurring check-in loop tied to real-world momentum.

### 3. AI Taglines — The Magic Hook
- Restore Claude-generated taglines. Each reload can produce a new angle.
- "Generate another take" button for users who want options.
- Taglines become a lightweight content lottery: users return to see what the AI says next.

### 4. Social Proof & Discovery
- **Trending Posters** feed: a public gallery of the most-viewed and most-shared posters this week.
- **Weekly Digest**: email or in-app summary of top repos in your language/stack and how your posters performed.
- **Notifications**: when someone shares or embeds your poster, you know about it.

### 5. Watermark as Funnel
- Every PNG includes a subtle "Made with Poster" footer and link.
- Every share is a billboard that drives new users.
- No watermark = no flywheel.

### 6. GitHub Actions Integration
- A `poster-child-action` that refreshes the poster on every release, star milestone, or push to main.
- Distribution *is* retention for developer tools. If it lives in the CI pipeline, it lives in the workflow.

## v1.1 Feature Set

| Feature | Retention Mechanic | Moat Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| User gallery / history | Recurring destination | Data gravity |
| Star-threshold auto-refresh | Event-driven re-engagement | Contextual relevance |
| AI tagline generation (restored) | Novelty / curiosity | AI leverage |
| View & share analytics | Status / feedback loop | Data asset |
| "Made with Poster" watermark | Viral acquisition loop | Brand equity |
| Embeddable README widget | Distribution & visibility | Network effects |
| GitHub Action for CI refresh | Workflow integration | Switching cost |
| Trending / discovery feed | Content consumption | Community gravity |
| Weekly digest | Habit formation | Engagement data |

## Success Metrics for v1.1

- **7-day return rate** > 30%
- **Posters per user** > 2 (not 1-and-done)
- **Organic share rate** (watermark click-through) > 5%
- **GitHub Action installs** > 100 repos in first month

## Bottom Line

Give the skeleton a heartbeat. Make it a show people binge, not a poster they pin once and forget.
