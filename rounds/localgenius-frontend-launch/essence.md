# Essence

**What this is:**
Tool that makes small business owners sleep at night because they'll never miss a customer question again.

**The feeling:**
Relief. "It already works." Peace of mind.

**The one thing that must be perfect:**
60-second setup that feels magical. Auto-detects business, pre-populates FAQs, shows live preview. User clicks one button.

**Creative direction:**
Invisible until needed. Indispensable when used.

---

**What to ship Week 1:**
Chat widget + minimal onboarding + FAQ editor. Nothing else.

**What makes it spread:**
Design quality that earns pride. Business owners show colleagues at chamber meetings. "Powered by LocalGenius" footer only works if product looks insanely great.

**Pricing model:**
$29/month flat. No usage limits. No freemium anxiety. Selling peace of mind, not metered software.

**The trap to avoid:**
Building features for users you don't have yet. Ship functional, iterate to magical.
