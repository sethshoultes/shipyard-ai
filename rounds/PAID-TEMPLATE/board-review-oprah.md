# Board Review — PAID-TEMPLATE (Oprah Winfrey)

**Verdict: Ship it, but look at who's not in the room.**

- First-5-minutes: Night-and-day win. 193-line YAML monster → 5-question conversation. New user breathes. Feels invited, not interrogated.
- Emotional resonance: Present. "Feel understood, not processed" — that's a value, not a feature. Whispered signature = dignity. This team has a heart.
- Trust: Recommend with confidence. Money lives in ops/, builds live in schema/. Clean boundaries = clean conscience. Guardrails prevent bait-and-switch. Tests hold promises accountable.
- Accessibility: Blind spot. No WCAG path, no screen-reader strategy, no cognitive-load design in intake. i18n banned at the door — locks out non-English speakers. Small business owners in India, Nigeria, Latin America can't walk in.
- Who's left out: Non-native English speakers. Visually impaired users. Anyone needing cognitive clarity in form flow.

**Score: 7/10** — Brilliant mechanics, incomplete humanity. Add accessibility to Wave 1. Then it's a 9.
