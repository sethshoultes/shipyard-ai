# Steve Jobs — Design & Brand Vision
## LocalGenius Lite / Round 1

---

## Product Naming: Kill "Lite"

**Name it: SPARK**

"LocalGenius Lite" sounds like diet soda. Weak. Apologetic. "Lite" means "we have a better version you can't afford."

SPARK is what happens when someone finds the answer they need. SPARK is instant. SPARK is magic. One syllable. Memorable. Ownable.

"Add Spark to your website." Not "add LocalGenius Lite embed widget version 1.2."

---

## Design Philosophy: Invisible Until Needed

The best design is no design. This isn't a chat widget — it's a **sixth sense for your website**.

The button disappears into the corner like it was always supposed to be there. When someone needs help, it appears exactly when they're about to leave. Not before. Not after. **Just in time.**

No loading spinners. No "Let me connect you." No fake typing indicators. The answer streams in like thought itself. Fast. Confident. Real.

We're not building software here — we're building **intuition as a service**.

---

## User Experience: The First 30 Seconds

You paste one line of code. You refresh your website. **Nothing happens.**

Good.

Then you scroll down, wondering if it worked, and you see it — a tiny circle in the corner, pulsing once like it's breathing. You click it.

The panel opens faster than you can blink. No splash screen. No "Welcome to LocalGenius Lite powered by Claude 3.5!" Just a simple question: *"What would you like to know?"*

You type: "What are your hours?" The answer appears instantly, pulled from your own page content, formatted beautifully, **accurate**. You close the panel. It disappears.

**That's it. That's the whole experience. Magic.**

## Brand Voice: A Confident Whisper

We don't shout. Intercom shouts. Drift shouts. "HEY! CHAT WITH US! LIMITED TIME OFFER!"

We whisper. "I'm here if you need me."

Our voice is:
- **Assured, not arrogant** — "Yes, I can answer that" not "Let me try to help!"
- **Direct, not chatty** — Give the answer. No small talk.
- **Invisible, not branded** — No "Great question!" No "Thanks for asking!" Just answers.

If the AI were a person, it's the brilliant librarian who knows exactly which book you need before you finish asking.

---

## What to Say NO To

### NO to branding everywhere
Remove "Powered by LocalGenius" from the free tier. Make it optional. Confidence doesn't need a billboard.

### NO to customization (MVP)
No color pickers. No font dropdowns. No "upload your logo here." Auto-detect the site's vibe and match it. **Taste as a feature.**

### NO to conversation history (MVP)
Each question is fresh. No "As I mentioned earlier..." No session memory. Stateless is simpler. Stateless is faster. **Simplicity ships.**

### NO to lead capture before chat
Don't put a gate in front of value. Let people ask questions. If they love it, they'll come back. Desperation repels. **Generosity converts.**

### NO to analytics dashboards (MVP)
You don't need a graph of "Top 10 Questions Asked." You need to ship tonight. Build metrics later, when you have users to measure.

## The Emotional Hook: Effortless Power

People will love this because **it makes them feel smart** without making them work.

Every website owner has anxiety: "What if someone has a question and can't find the answer?" This solves it in 30 seconds. No training. No maintenance. No thinking.

It's the **"it just works"** feeling. The same feeling you get when you plug in AirPods and they connect instantly. The same feeling when you drop a file in iCloud and it appears on all your devices.

**You paste the script. Intelligence appears. That's the magic.**
