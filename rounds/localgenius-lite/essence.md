# SPARK — Essence

**What it really is:**
Intelligence that makes websites instantly helpful without requiring anyone to do anything.

**The feeling:**
Effortless power. "I pasted one line and my site got smarter."

**The one thing that must be perfect:**
First 3 seconds after clicking the button — smooth open, instant response, zero friction.

**Creative direction:**
Invisible until magical.

---

## Core Tension (Resolved)

Steve: Polish the experience, earn word-of-mouth through craft.
Elon: Ship fast, iterate from real usage data.

**Answer:** Both. Fast AND great.

## What Ships

Widget that feels like it belongs to the user's site, not ours.
Clean gradient, smooth animation, streaming responses.
Powered-by footer (removable for pay).
No dashboard, no billing, no admin.
Client-side UUIDs, zero backend.

## What Dies

"LocalGenius Lite" name.
Exit-intent detection.
Auto-vibe matching.
Lead capture gates.
Dashboards before users.
