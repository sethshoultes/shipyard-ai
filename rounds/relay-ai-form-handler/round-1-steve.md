# Relay — Chief Design & Brand Officer Review

## Product Naming

**Relay.** One word. No modifiers. No "Pro," no "AI," no "Suite." You don't buy "iPhone Advanced Communication Device" — you buy iPhone. Relay is a verb and a promise: "I receive, I understand, I pass to the right person." If you need three words to explain what you do, you've already lost. The name is the user manual.

## Design Philosophy

This is not a dashboard. It's a command center where leads land and decisions happen. Every pixel must answer one question: *Where does this message need to go?* If a button, a chart, or a "workflow template" doesn't help a business owner route a message faster, it doesn't belong. Think Lincoln Center, not Office Depot. We are not building software — we are sculpting focus. The design is done not when there's nothing left to add, but when there's nothing left to remove.

## User Experience (First 30 Seconds)

A small business owner installs Relay. She opens wp-admin. She sees exactly one thing: messages, categorized, color-coded, alive. No setup wizard asking for her life story. No "configure 47 preferences." The first form submission hits, and within seconds she sees a red badge: **SALES — HIGH URGENCY** — with a one-click reply. She doesn't "learn" Relay. She experiences it, the way you instinctively knew how the iPod scroll wheel worked the moment your thumb touched it.

## Brand Voice

Relay doesn't "leverage AI to optimize cross-functional workflows." It says: *"This one goes to Sales. This one is spam. Done."* We speak like the world's smartest assistant who actually respects your time — short sentences, no buzzwords, no explaining. Confident, human clarity. If it sounds like it could be printed on a whitepaper from a company that sells enterprise seat licenses, burn it and start over.

## What to Say NO To

NO to feature creep. NO to "maybe someone wants a Gutenberg block" — let WordPress handle forms; we handle what happens AFTER. NO to CSV export in v1. NO to "admin themes." NO to Slack routing until the core routing is flawless. NO to every checkbox that turns Relay into a Swiss Army knife when what people need is a scalpel. We ship one thing that works magically, not ten things that work adequately. Simplicity means removing until you are terrified, then removing one more thing.

## The Emotional Hook

People will love Relay because it gives them back the one thing every small business owner loses: the feeling of control. Right now, leads disappear into the inbox abyss — it's like pouring water into a cracked cup. Relay makes every single submission visible, classified, and impossible to ignore. It's not a plugin. It's peace of mind wearing an orange badge.
