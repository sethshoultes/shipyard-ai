# Shonda Retention Roadmap — Relay AI Form Handler v1.1
**Owner:** Narrative & Retention
**Mission:** Turn utility plumbing into a product users binge.

---

## Core Insight

Users don’t abandon Relay because it fails. They abandon it because they never feel it succeed. The classification happens in a cron job in an empty room. There is no inciting incident, no aha moment witnessed live, no reason to open the app tomorrow. v1.1 must give the product a pulse.

---

## What Keeps Users Coming Back

### 1. The Pilot Episode Onboarding
Users need to see the magic in real time, not read about it in a manual.

- **Guided First Form:** On install, auto-create a sample contact form and inject a demo submission.
- **Live Classification Theater:** Real-time “Claude is analyzing…” animation with progressive reveal — intent, confidence score, route assignment.
- **Raw vs. Classified Preview:** Side-by-side diff so the user sees the transformation, not just the output.
- **Gold Moment:** If the demo submission is flagged as high-value, trigger a celebratory “Gold detected!” animation. Make the first win feel personal.

### 2. Emotional Cliffhangers in the Inbox
Replace silent background routing with narrative tension.

- **Classification Preview Pane:** Before a lead is routed, show it in a “Claude’s Verdict” card with suspenseful copy — *“Looks like sales… confidence 94%… routing to Slack.”*
- **Spam vs. Gold Drama:** A weekly “What Claude Caught” digest highlighting the best lead and the worst spam. Users should feel relief and triumph, not just efficiency.
- **Status Transition Beats:** Expand the micro-dopamine of color-coded badges. Animate pending → classified → routed. Each transition is a tiny narrative win.

### 3. The Weekly Lead Report (Email Retention Anchor)
An email users actually want.

- **Subject line rotation:** *“This week Claude found 3 gold leads”* / *“You were spared 47 spam messages”* / *“Your busiest lead day was Wednesday.”*
- **Body structure:**
  - Hero stat: Time saved this week.
  - Lead of the week: Best classified submission with a pull quote.
  - Spam blocked: A satisfying number.
  - Streak/milestone: *“7-day classification streak 🔥”*
  - CTA: *“Review your lead board →”*

### 4. Time Saved Scoreboard
Make utility emotional.

- Dashboard widget: *“You’ve saved 4.2 hours this month. That’s one full afternoon.”*
- Milestones: *“10 hours saved” → “25 hours” → “You’ve basically hired a virtual assistant.”*
- Agency tier: Cross-site “Season Recap” showing cumulative hours across all managed properties.

### 5. Voice, Character, and Copy Personality
Eliminate server-log tone.

- Replace *“Pending classifications: 0”* with *“All caught up. Claude’s sipping coffee.”*
- Replace *“Classification complete”* with *“Verdict delivered.”*
- Give Relay a point of view: sharp, efficient, slightly protective of the user’s time.

---

## Anti-Goals (What We Won’t Do)

- **More admin CSS polish.** If it doesn’t increase return rate, it ships after retention is proven.
- **Deeper configuration options.** Config hell kills onboarding; every new setting must be justified by a narrative beat.
- **Passive dashboard widgets.** A number sitting on a screen is not a story. Animate it, contextualize it, or cut it.
