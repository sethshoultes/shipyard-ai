# ESSENCE

## What This Really Is
WordPress plugin that makes websites feel alive by answering visitor questions instantly.

## The Feeling
Pride. "My site just got smarter than my competitor's."

## The One Thing That Must Be Perfect
Zero-config installation. Click activate, it works. No setup, no keys, no bullshit.

## Creative Direction
Invisible until needed. Instant. Human.

---

## Stripped Truth

**Name:** Not "WP-Agent" (technical garbage). Not "GUIDE" (lawsuit bait). Something human that passes trademark.

**What it does:** Chat widget pulls site content, answers visitor questions via Claude. One model. No memory. No analytics. Just answers.

**Why people install:** Remove friction. Stop getting calls about info "right there on the site."

**Icon:** Compass or chat bubble. Decide with first 100 users, not in debate.

**Tech:** Vanilla JS widget (<20KB) + WordPress plugin + async AI proxy + Claude Haiku. No React. No embeddings. No Cloudflare Workers day 1.

**Success metric:** User activates plugin, never touches settings, gets email from customer saying "I love the chat thing on your site."
