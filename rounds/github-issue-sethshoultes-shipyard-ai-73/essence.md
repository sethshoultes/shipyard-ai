# Essence

**What this is:** Infrastructure that makes sandboxed plugins actually work in production.

**The feeling:** Relief. Zero surprise. It just works.

**The one thing that must be perfect:** Invisibility. Developers shouldn't know this exists.

**Creative direction:** Auto-inject. Build-time validation. Zero config.

---

**Tension:**
- Elon: Ship the one-line fix in 2 minutes
- Steve: Auto-detect and inject at build time

**Synthesis:**
Ship binding immediately. Add auto-injection within days, not months.

**Non-negotiable:**
Fail at build time, never at runtime. No silent failures. No debugging archaeology.

**The real product:**
Not the binding. The promise that plugins work. Keep the promise or kill plugins entirely.
