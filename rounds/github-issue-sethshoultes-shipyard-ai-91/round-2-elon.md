# Round 2: Rebuttal

Steve, I love the vision. "Promptfolio" is a fine name. "Show your work" is exactly right. But we're debating execution, not poetry — and poetry is expensive.

## Where Beauty Blocks Shipping

Your "drag your Claude export onto the page" moment is a maintenance trap. Those export schemas are undocumented and shift quarterly. You will spend 30% of your engineering time chasing OpenAI's JSON schema drift instead of building product. The "Kunsthalle" metaphor sounds incredible in a keynote, but you're shipping inside 50,000 arbitrary WordPress themes. One exquisite layout does not survive `functions.php`. If you can't render perfectly in a $5 Bluehost theme with 47 other plugins, your gallery is a broken slideshow.

"Midnight gallery with spotlights" requires design iteration you don't have time for in a single agent session. Dignity ships after product-market fit.

## Why Simplicity Wins

Technical simplicity is not the enemy of beauty — it is the foundation. A React app on Vercel with one immaculate template gives you full control over the canvas. Edge-cached static HTML means zero database reads, zero theme conflicts, and global speed. You can still generate the beautiful OG images and embeddable cards Steve wants. The difference is: it works before breakfast, scales to dinner, and doesn't collapse when someone tweets it.

Manual Markdown/JSON paste is robust. Optional WP export keeps the ecosystem happy without making it your prison. Decoupling is not betrayal of WordPress — it is survival.

## Conceded: Taste Matters

Steve is right on three things: one template only, no barnacles (SEO widgets, popups, chatbots), and brand voice. The "Show your work" ethos should permeate every pixel. Auto-generated shareable images are non-negotiable — they are the growth engine. That is where taste and technical correctness align perfectly.

## Non-Negotiables

1. **SaaS-first, WP-optional.** Host the beautiful part yourself. Syndicate to WordPress, don't architect inside it.
2. **Zero dynamic widgets in v1.** No "Try this prompt." No API keys. No inference cost center before revenue.
3. **One React template, edge-cached static, automatic OG image generation.** Ship the monument. Scale the monument. Don't theme-engine the monument.

Steve built the cathedral. I'm pouring the foundation. Foundations first.
