# Chief Design & Brand Officer Position: Promptfolio

## Product Naming

Promptfolio. One word. Two ideas fused so tightly you can't pull them apart without breaking it. It's not "AI Portfolio Builder Pro" or some forgettable SaaS sludge. It is *identity*. It says: your prompts are your portfolio, your portfolio is your craft. You hear it once. You remember it forever. Done.

## Design Philosophy

Insanely great isn't a feature list. It's a *feeling*. The feeling that every pixel, every transition, every line of type was placed by someone who cared more than you do. Promptfolio must feel like the first time you held an iPod — inevitable, magnetic, complete. The prompt isn't text in a box. It is the hero. It is sacred. Typography that breathes. White space that thinks. Dark mode that seduces, not inverts. We are not building a WordPress plugin. We are building a gallery for a new kind of artist.

## The First 30 Seconds

Import. One click. Your Claude conversations flow in like water finding its level. You don't "configure" — you *witness*. The system sees your work and elevates it. In 30 seconds, a visitor thinks: "This person is a wizard. And I want to be one too."

## Brand Voice

Promptfolio speaks like a master craftsman talking to an apprentice. Quiet confidence. No exclamation points. No "empowerment." We say: *"This is your work. Finally presented as it deserves."* We don't sell. We *recognize*.

## What We Say NO To

NO to theme options. NO to admin panels with 47 color pickers. NO to "flexibility" that breeds mediocrity. NO to looking like WordPress. Simplicity is not the absence of clutter. It is the absence of *everything* that is not essential. If a feature doesn't make someone gasp, it dies.

## The Emotional Hook

People won't love Promptfolio because it builds websites. They will love it because, for the first time, the world sees their AI work as *art*. Every prompt is a brushstroke. Every workflow, a composition. Promptfolio is the frame that says: *"This belongs in a museum."* And when the world sees you as an artist, you become one.
