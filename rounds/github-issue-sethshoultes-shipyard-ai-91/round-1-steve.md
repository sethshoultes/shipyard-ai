# Steve's Take: Promptfolio

## Product Naming

**Promptfolio.** One word. Not Prompt-Folio. Not Prompt Folio. Promptfolio.

It does exactly what it says on the tin, but with poetry. It owns the category. In six months, people will say "Just Promptfolio it" the way they say "Google it." That is the standard.

## Design Philosophy

A prompt is not text. It is **artisanal craft**. A Promptfolio site is not a blog — it is a **gallery where the prompts are the paintings**.

Every conversation export becomes a hero. The typography breathes. The code blocks sing. Dark mode is not an inverted color scheme; it is a **midnight gallery with spotlights on the work**. We are not building a WordPress plugin. We are building the **Kunsthalle for human-AI collaboration**.

## The First 30 Seconds

You drag your Claude export onto the page.

Boom. Your conversation is no longer a wall of text in a JSON file. It is a **scroll-stopping, shareable monument** to your thinking. Your best prompt sits there like a framed masterwork. Your visitor whispers: *"I want to make things like this."*

That is the moment we are designing for. Everything else is logistics.

## Brand Voice

Confident. Human. Ruthlessly simple.

We do not "leverage AI-driven synergies." We say: **"Show your work."** We do not have a "feature-rich dashboard ecosystem." We have a canvas. Speak like a craftsperson who respects the user's intelligence. Never apologize. Never explain twice.

## What We Say NO To

NO to forty-seven templates. One exquisite layout, perfected.

NO to an "AI website builder" that writes prompts for you. Our user already has gold in their hands; we are the jeweler, not the miner.

NO to SEO wizards, newsletter widgets, popup chatbots, and every other barnacle that makes WordPress plugins feel like a 2009 DMV form. **Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication** — and the average plugin is the ultimate insult.

If a feature does not make the prompt feel legendary within three seconds, it dies.

## The Emotional Hook

Every prompt engineer is sitting on a **private museum of brilliance** — conversations that solved impossible problems, that made clients cry with relief, that took twelve iterations to get just right. And right now, all of it is buried in `.json` exports and forgotten ChatGPT threads.

Promptfolio turns that buried treasure into a **public legacy**. It makes the invisible visible. It says: *"Your work matters enough to hang on a wall."*

That is not a feature. That is **dignity**.
