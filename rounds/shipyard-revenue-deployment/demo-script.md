# Shipyard Revenue Deployment — 2-Minute Demo Script

**Total read time:** ~2:00
**Voice:** Human narrator. Stressed agency owner. Not a pitch deck.

---

NARRATOR: Your client called at 11:47. Site's down. SSL expired two days ago. They want to know why they're paying you.

[SCREEN: Phone lock screen. Incoming call: "Mike — Client." Finger swipes Decline.]

NARRATOR: You didn't see it coming because you're building the next site. Not babysitting the last one. But the last one? That's your reputation.

[SCREEN: Laptop. Shipyard marketing page. Three pricing cards snap into view: Keep, Grow, Scale.]

NARRATOR: So you stop building alone. You start shipping with Shipyard. Three tiers. Clear hierarchy. No annual toggle nobody asked for. Client clicks. Stripe Checkout. Done.

[SCREEN: Stripe Checkout. Email field auto-filled. "Subscribe" button. Confetti-free success state.]

NARRATOR: Thirty seconds later the webhook hits D1. Customer record. Tier locked. Portal token generated.

[SCREEN: Gmail inbox. New message: "Your Shipyard portal is live." Cursor clicks link.]

NARRATOR: No password screen. No magic link. No "forgot your mother's maiden name." A tokenized URL that just knows.

[SCREEN: Portal dashboard. Site card: "Acme Co." Three badges appear in sequence.]

[SCREEN: Uptime — GREEN. SSL — GREEN. Lighthouse — YELLOW.]

NARRATOR: Green means breathing. Yellow means look soon. Red means you already knew, because the checker—uptime, SSL, Lighthouse—runs every six hours and writes to D1 before your client notices.

[SCREEN: Resend dashboard. "Welcome" delivered. "Day-7 check-in" queued with cron icon.]

NARRATOR: Day seven an email lands. Not sales. A check-in. "Anything broken we should know about?" It sends itself. You sleep.

[SCREEN: Portal refreshes. Badges hold steady. Revenue counter ticks: $1,847 MRR.]

NARRATOR: You didn't build auth. You didn't build a billing portal. You built recurring revenue, client trust, and eight hours of sleep.

[SCREEN: Phone shows 7:00 AM. Zero missed calls. Lock screen fades to black.]

NARRATOR: That's the wow. Not the feature list. The silence.

[SCREEN: Shipyard wordmark. Tagline: "Ship it. Sleep better."]

---

**End.**
