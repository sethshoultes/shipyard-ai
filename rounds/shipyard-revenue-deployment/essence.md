# Essence

Product: Recurring peace of mind for website owners.

Feeling: Customer feels smart, not anxious. Invisible work made visible.

One thing: Trust must be tangible. A green badge. A proactive email. Proof they didn't waste money.

Creative direction: Brutal simplicity, undeniable trust.

---

- Strip to: payment + proof + contact.
- Portal over email threads. Health score over manual reports.
- No auth rabbit holes. No tracking tables. No cargo-cult SaaS UI.
- One click to subscribe. One glance to feel safe.

Build the feeling. Revenue follows.
