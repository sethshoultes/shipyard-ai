# Anchor — Demo Script
### Runtime: 2 minutes

---

**[SCREEN: Black. Then slowly fade up on a browser tab — a beautiful new website. Confetti animation plays. "Site is Live!" toast notification.]**

NARRATOR: You did it. After months of late nights, endless revisions, and one too many arguments about button colors — your site is finally live.

**[SCREEN: Quick montage — the owner checking their phone, refreshing the page, showing a friend. Smiles all around.]**

NARRATOR: You check it obsessively. Everything looks perfect. Your visitors are coming in. Life is good.

**[SCREEN: Calendar pages flip. Day 3. Day 7. Day 14. The browser tab slowly collects dust in a sea of other tabs.]**

NARRATOR: Then life takes over.

**[SCREEN: The site, untouched. Meanwhile, a small red badge appears: "Plugin update available." Then another. A page load spinner hangs just a beat too long.]**

NARRATOR: You assume everything's fine. Because why wouldn't it be? But somewhere, a third-party script is slowing things down. Your mobile score is dropping. Your visitors are bouncing.

**[SCREEN: A frustrated user on their phone, watching the loading bar crawl. They tap away.]**

NARRATOR: And you have no idea.

**[SCREEN: Hard cut to the Anchor landing page. Clean. Simple. "Someone's got your back."]**

NARRATOR: This is Anchor.

**[SCREEN: A new customer signs up. Just an email and a URL. That's it.]**

NARRATOR: You give us your site. That's all we need.

**[SCREEN: Behind the scenes visualization — elegant, minimal. A cron job fires. Monday, 3am UTC. PageSpeed API calls go out.]**

NARRATOR: Every Monday morning, while you're still asleep, Anchor runs a full performance analysis. Mobile. Desktop. Core Web Vitals. The metrics that actually matter.

**[SCREEN: A beautiful email arrives in an inbox. Subject line: "Welcome to Anchor." Personal, warm — not a marketing blast.]**

NARRATOR: On day one, you get a welcome note. Not a pitch deck. Not an upsell. Just... someone saying hello.

**[SCREEN: One week later. Another email: "Your first performance report is ready." Inside: Mobile 78, Desktop 92. A simple tip about image compression.]**

NARRATOR: Week one, your first report arrives. Here's your score. Here's one thing you can do to improve it. That's it.

**[SCREEN: Time passes. Monthly email. Quarterly refresh. The customer barely has to think about it.]**

NARRATOR: Month after month, Anchor watches. You don't log into dashboards. You don't configure alerts. You just... live your life.

**[SCREEN: Dashboard view showing trend line — the score climbing slowly from 78 to 91 over three months.]**

NARRATOR: And quietly, in the background, your site gets healthier.

**[SCREEN: Close-up on an email. Subject: "90 days of keeping watch." Inside: "I've run 13 performance checks. Your mobile score is up 12 points. Here's your quarterly snapshot."]**

NARRATOR: Then one morning, ninety days in, an email arrives. And for the first time, you realize:

**[SCREEN: Pull back to reveal the customer at their desk, coffee in hand, smiling at the email. Relaxed.]**

NARRATOR: You haven't worried about your website in three months.

**[SCREEN: The Anchor logo. Then the tagline fades in beneath it.]**

NARRATOR: Anchor. Someone's got your back.

**[SCREEN: "Basic: $29/month. Pro: $79/month. No dashboards. No logins. Just peace of mind."]**

NARRATOR: *(softer, almost like an afterthought)* No dashboards. No notifications to ignore. Just someone watching — so you don't have to.

**[SCREEN: Fade to black. Anchor logo lingers.]**

**END**
