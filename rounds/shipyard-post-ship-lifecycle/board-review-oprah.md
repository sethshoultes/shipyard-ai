## Trust: Would I Recommend This?

**Yes—with one caveat.**

**What works:**
- Emails are human, not robotic
- Cadence is respectful (5 emails/year, not weekly spam)
- Clear unsubscribe (respects autonomy)
- Reply-to goes to real person (Phil), not support@
- "Homeport" brand signals commitment

**What concerns me:**
- Technical complexity creates launch risk
- 24-48 hour DNS propagation = delays
- Manual CSV upload for MVP = friction
- No proof emails actually work yet (just templates + guides)

**The gap:** System designed beautifully, but not yet *proven*.

I'd recommend this after seeing first batch delivered + customer replies.

Right now it's a promise. Great promises need proof.

---

## Accessibility: Who's Being Left Out?

**People who need simplicity get drowned in detail.**

Config-decisions.md: 178 lines on email addresses and DNS.
Resend-setup-guide.md: 706 lines on technical setup.

Who's left out:
- Non-technical founders who'd implement this
- Small teams without DevOps expertise
- Anyone who needs "click 3 buttons, done"

This system requires:
- DNS provider access
- Understanding of SPF/DKIM/DMARC
- Command-line comfort
- Patience for 48-hour propagation

**Who this serves:** Technical teams with infrastructure resources.

**Who gets excluded:** Solo founders, small agencies, non-technical users.

**Fix needed:** Simpler onboarding path. Video walkthrough. Pre-configured templates. One-click setup option.

This is *good work*.

Make it easier to open.
