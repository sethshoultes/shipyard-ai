# Essence

**What this product is:**
Selling permission to stop worrying about your website.

**The feeling:**
Invisible until essential. Sleep. Trust without surveillance.

**The one thing that must be perfect:**
Proof of care when something breaks — not dashboards when nothing does.

**Creative direction:**
Calm pilot during turbulence.

---

**Tensions resolved:**
- Steve wins: Voice, emotion, "Care" branding, email-first UX
- Elon wins: Token pricing, referral distribution, horizontal scaling
- Both wrong: Monthly monitoring is theater; incident reports are proof

**The synthesis:**
$500/month = 100K tokens. Dedicated capacity. Silence when healthy, 3-line fix report when broken. Referral credits for growth. No dashboards. No anxiety. Just care.

**Non-negotiables:**
- Token budgets (honest) over revision rounds (fake scarcity)
- Incident logging (accountability) over health monitoring (theater)
- Referral credits (viral loop) baked in from day 1
