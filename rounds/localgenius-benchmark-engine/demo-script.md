# LocalGenius RANK — Demo Script
### Runtime: 2 minutes

---

**NARRATOR:**
You ever wake up and check your phone before your feet hit the floor?

[SCREEN: Close-up of a hand reaching for a phone on a nightstand, 6:47 AM]

**NARRATOR:**
Maria owns a taco shop in Austin. Fourteen-hour days. Best al pastor in the city—and that's not her opinion, that's her customers'. But here's the thing about running a small business...

[SCREEN: Maria's phone, scrolling through a sea of review notifications, Google alerts, competitor social posts—chaos]

**NARRATOR:**
...you never actually know where you stand. You get reviews—good, bad, cryptic. You see the line at the place down the street. You *feel* like you're doing okay. But feeling isn't knowing.

[SCREEN: Split screen—Maria's taco shop with a decent lunch crowd, a competitor's shop with a longer line]

**NARRATOR:**
And knowing matters. Because when you don't know, you can't improve. When you don't know, you can't prioritize. When you don't know... you just work harder and hope.

[SCREEN: Maria closing up late, exhausted, turning off the lights]

**NARRATOR:**
That's the problem. Now here's what we built.

---

[SCREEN: LocalGenius dashboard, clean interface. The RANK widget loads—a large, bold "#7"]

**NARRATOR:**
This is RANK. One number. Your number. Maria logs in and she sees it: Number seven. Out of forty-three restaurants in Austin.

[SCREEN: The widget fully rendered—"#7 of 43" with "Austin Restaurants" as the cohort label. A small green arrow shows "+2" from last week]

**NARRATOR:**
Not seven out of all the restaurants in Texas—we're not here to depress anyone. Seven out of her actual competition. The restaurants in her city, in her category. The ones stealing her customers *right now*.

[SCREEN: Percentile bar fills to show "Top 17%"]

**NARRATOR:**
And look—she moved up two spots since last week. That's not luck. That's data.

---

[SCREEN: User clicks "Why this rank?" — the breakdown expands]

**NARRATOR:**
She clicks "Why this rank?" and now she's not guessing anymore. Review count: strong. Average rating: crushing it. Review velocity—that's how many new reviews she's getting—also good.

[SCREEN: Component scores visible: Review Count 82, Avg Rating 91, Review Velocity 78, Response Rate 45, Response Time 38]

**NARRATOR:**
But look at this. Response rate: forty-five. Response time: thirty-eight. Those are... not great.

[SCREEN: Highlight on the lower scores, red/amber color coding]

**NARRATOR:**
And this is the part the other guys can't see. Google doesn't tell her competitors how fast she responds to reviews. But we know. Because she responds through LocalGenius. That's *our* data. And it's telling her exactly where to focus.

---

[SCREEN: The insight section below the rank—amber background, text reads: "2 reviews away from #6. Respond to your 3 pending reviews to boost your response rate."]

**NARRATOR:**
See that? "Two reviews away from number six." Not "improve your metrics." Not "consider optimizing your engagement." Two reviews. A number she can hit before lunch.

[SCREEN: Maria's expression changes—recognition, determination]

**NARRATOR:**
So she does. She responds to those three pending reviews right there in the app. Takes her four minutes.

[SCREEN: Quick montage—Maria typing thoughtful responses, hitting send, send, send]

---

[SCREEN: One week later. The RANK widget loads again. "#5" appears with a green "+2" arrow]

**NARRATOR:**
One week later.

[SCREEN: The full widget—"#5 of 43" — "Top 12%" — trend line showing steady climb over 4 weeks]

**NARRATOR:**
Number five.

[SCREEN: The trend line sparkline, green, climbing from #9 to #5 over four weeks]

**NARRATOR:**
Not because she worked harder. Because she worked *smarter*. Because for the first time, she knew exactly where she stood and exactly what to do about it.

[SCREEN: Wide shot—Maria's taco shop, lunch rush, line out the door]

**NARRATOR:**
That's RANK. One number. Your number. And your next move.

[SCREEN: LocalGenius logo. Tagline: "Know where you stand."]

**NARRATOR:**
Know where you stand.
