# Essence

**What it is:**
A daily heartbeat notification that makes restaurant owners feel their business is alive and someone is watching.

**The feeling:**
Hope, not dread, when you check your phone.

**The one thing:**
First 30 seconds. Show something meaningful immediately or fail.

**Creative direction:**
Whisper. Ship fast. Measure.

---

**Core tension:**
Steve wants companionship. Elon wants conversion. Both are right.

**Synthesis:**
- Warm copy (Steve)
- Simple 3-table architecture (Elon)
- One beautiful insight per day (Steve)
- Shareable viral badges (Elon)
- No aggressive upsells (Steve)
- Pre-computed, scales to 1M (Elon)

**Strip everything else.**
