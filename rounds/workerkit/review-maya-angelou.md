# Copy Review: create-workerkit

## Does it feel human?
Almost. Some lines still corporate.

## Is there rhythm?
Inconsistent. First half punches. Second half drags.

## Does the headline stop you?
Yes. "Zero-to-deployed business app in under 60 seconds" — bold claim, clear promise.

## What's trying too hard?
- "production-ready" appears 3x (line 5, 37, 51)
- Checkmarks (✅) feel like LinkedIn
- "Built for founders and indie hackers who want to ship fast" — reads like positioning doc, not truth

## The 3 weakest lines rewritten:

**Original (line 5):**
> A production-ready scaffold generator for full-stack AI applications on Cloudflare Workers. Answer 5 questions, get a deployable app with authentication, database, AI integration, and payments pre-wired.

**Rewrite:**
> Answer 5 questions. Get a working app with auth, database, AI, and payments already talking to each other.

---

**Original (line 103):**
> **Zero dependencies** in the CLI - your generated projects own 100% of their code

**Rewrite:**
> No dependencies. The code is yours. All of it.

---

**Original (line 167):**
> **Built for founders and indie hackers who want to ship fast.**

**Rewrite:**
> For people who have something to build.
