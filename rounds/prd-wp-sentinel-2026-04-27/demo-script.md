# WP Sentinel — 2-Minute Demo Script

---

NARRATOR:
It's 3:47 AM. Launch in six hours. Your WordPress site just threw a "critical issue" that might as well say "good luck."

[SCREEN: Black. Phone lock screen shows 3:47 AM. Notification banner: "WordPress Site Health: Critical."]

NARRATOR:
Seventeen tabs. Forums. Stack Overflow. A $200-a-month retainer hitting voicemail. You're not a sysadmin. You sell pottery.

[SCREEN: Rapid montage of browser tabs — WordPress support forums, Stack Overflow question, hosting support page.]

NARRATOR:
You log into wp-admin. Wall of widgets. But today—color. Red. Yellow. Green. Like traffic lights, except they mean something.

[SCREEN: wp-admin dashboard loads. New widget: "Sentinel Health." Five color-coded bars. PHP: yellow. Plugins: red. Permalinks: green. Cache: yellow. Thumbnails: red.]

NARRATOR:
PHP's old. Plugins screaming. Thumbnails broken since that migration three weeks ago. You knew about none of this.

[SCREEN: Cursor hovers over red "Plugins" bar. Tooltip: "3 updates required." Hovers over red "Thumbnails." Tooltip: "Regeneration recommended since April 3rd migration."]

NARRATOR:
You click the chat bubble in the corner.

[SCREEN: Bottom-right of wp-admin. A chat bubble button pulses. Cursor clicks. Slide-over panel opens from the right edge.]

NARRATOR:
"Why are my thumbnails broken?"

[SCREEN: Chat panel. User message appears. Typing indicator bounces.]

NARRATOR:
No FAQ. No copy-paste. It gives you *your* answer. Because it sees your site.

[SCREEN: AI message renders: "Your thumbnails weren't regenerated after your April 3rd server migration. Want me to fix it?"]

NARRATOR:
You didn't attach logs. Didn't open a ticket. It just knew.

[SCREEN: Blue action button appears below AI message: "Regenerate Thumbnails."]

NARRATOR:
And it can fix it. But it won't touch your site without permission.

[SCREEN: Cursor clicks button. Confirmation modal slides up: "Regenerate thumbnails? This uses a reversible WordPress API and cannot delete images."]

NARRATOR:
Two clicks. Not one. Your site. Your rules.

[SCREEN: Cursor clicks "Confirm." Modal closes. Green success toast slides down: "Done. 47 images regenerated."]

NARRATOR:
Forty-seven images. Eight seconds.

[SCREEN: Health widget updates in real-time. Thumbnails bar flips from red to green.]

NARRATOR:
Plugin updates. PHP check. Cache clear. Every answer contextual. Every action reversible. No code. No shell. No retainer.

[SCREEN: Chat continues. Cursor triggers more actions—confirm modals, success toasts. Health bars turn green one by one.]

NARRATOR:
Health monitoring's free. Lives right inside wp-admin. AI needs a key, sure. But the peace of mind? Instant.

[SCREEN: "Tools → Sentinel" settings page. Clean form: Worker URL, API key. Two toggle switches.]

NARRATOR:
Six hours to launch. Site's green. No seventeenth tab. Back to bed.

[SCREEN: All five health bars green. Chat panel minimized. Clean wp-admin dashboard. Fade to black.]

NARRATOR:
WP Sentinel. Your site finally learned to talk. Now you know what it's saying.

---
*End.*
