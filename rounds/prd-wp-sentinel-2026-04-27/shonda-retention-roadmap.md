## Part 1: The Retention Hooks

### 1. Emotional Cliffhangers (Urgency as Entertainment)
A static widget is a dead scene. The dashboard must create suspense.

- **"3 plugins need attention" countdown badge** — red dot urgency that decays if ignored.
- **Score-drop notifications** — "Your security score fell from 94 to 71 overnight. Tap to see what changed."
- **Weekly cold opens** — Monday email: "Last week your site blocked 2 brute-force attempts. Here is what almost got in."
- **Season finale mechanics** — Monthly recap with a "Season Score" users want to beat next month.

### 2. Progress & Streaks (The Dopamine Timeline)
Users return to products that make them feel like protagonists.

- **Action history** — A scrollable timeline of every fix applied: "You flushed permalinks. You cleared cache. You updated WooCommerce."
- **Security streaks** — "32 days without a critical issue. Don't break the streak."
- **Before / after scores** — Every fix triggers a dramatic score jump with animation and positive reinforcement.
- **Achievement badges** — "Midnight Savior" (fixed an issue between 12am–4am), "Clean Slate" (30 days all-green).

### 3. Proactive Outreach (The Show Comes to Them)
If the user has to remember to open wp-admin, the story is already canceled.

- **Critical alerts via email** — Immediate, plain-language warnings with one-click "Fix it" deep links.
- **Weekly digest** — Not just problems. Celebrate wins: "Your site was healthy all week. Average load time: 0.8s. Top performing plugin: RankMath."
- **Positive reinforcement** — "All clear" notifications prevent the product from becoming associated only with panic.
- **Re-engagement arcs** — If a user hasn't logged in for 14 days: "Your site missed you. Here is what happened while you were away."

### 4. Chat Continuity (The AI Remembers)
A chat that resets every session is a guest star, not a series regular.

- **Conversation history** — The AI recalls last week's panic: "Last Tuesday you fixed broken permalinks. Want me to verify they're still stable?"
- **Follow-up prompts** — Proactive nudges based on prior context: "You asked about security headers. I can now scan for CSP violations."
- **Rich rendering** — Code blocks, diffs, formatted diagnostics. At 3 AM, plain text is cruelty. Clarity is kindness.
- **Relationship framing** — The AI refers to itself as the user's guardian. It should feel like a partner, not a search engine.

### 5. Social Proof & Sharing (Word-of-Mouth as Distribution)
The best retention is a friend who already uses it.

- **Shareable health score cards** — Branded images for Twitter/LinkedIn: "My site scored 98/100 this week. Guarded by Sentinel."
- **Hosting partner co-marketing** — "WP Engine + Sentinel" branded reports that make the host look good and Sentinel indispensable.
- **Team/multi-user arcs** — Agency dashboards showing client site health scores. The agency becomes the distribution channel.

## Part 2: v1.1 Features (The Next Season)

### Core Product
| Feature | Why It Retains | Board Alignment |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Proactive monitoring & scheduled scans** | The guardian should patrol, not wait to be called. | Steve: guardian duty; Elon: cut complexity, but polling is non-negotiable for retention. |
| **One-click fixes with undo** | Lowers the barrier to action; reduces fear of breaking something. | Buffett: reduces support cost; Shonda: creates "hero moment." |
| **Plugin update risk scoring** | Users avoid updates from fear. A risk score ("99% safe, 2,400 sites updated successfully") converts anxiety into confidence. | Jensen: data flywheel begins here. |
| **Rate-limited chat with usage dashboard** | Users should see their conversation budget like a phone data plan. Transparency builds trust. | Buffett: caps marginal cost; Steve: no unlimited bankruptcy. |
| **Conversation history & export** | Users invest in a product that remembers their context. | Shonda: continuity = commitment. |
| **Rich chat rendering (code blocks, diffs)** | At 3 AM, formatted output is the difference between panic and action. | Steve: non-negotiable for emotional response. |
| **Dark mode (when Core ships it)** | Follow WordPress Core. One perfect widget means no custom themes until Core provides the toggle. | Steve: "No settings" purity. |

### Retention Layer
| Feature | Narrative Function |
|---------|-------------------|

## The Retention Thesis

> Users do not return to WordPress plugins. They return to *relationships*.

WP Sentinel must evolve from a tool into a character: invisible when healthy, decisive when broken, remembered when the crisis is over. The health scanner is the cold open. The weekly recap is the hook. The chat history is the season arc.

**v1.1 is not a feature list. It is a second episode. Make them need to know what happens next.**
