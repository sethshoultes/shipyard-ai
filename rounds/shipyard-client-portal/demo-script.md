# Shipyard Client Portal - 2-Minute Demo Script

---

NARRATOR: You hire an AI to build your website.

[SCREEN: Empty email inbox, cursor hovering]

NARRATOR: Now what?

You wait. Check your email. Wait some more.

[SCREEN: Refresh email. Nothing. Clock ticking forward: 2 hours, 5 hours, 1 day]

NARRATOR: Is the AI working? Did it fail? Is your money gone?

You have no idea.

[SCREEN: Fade to black]

---

NARRATOR: This is the problem Shipyard solves.

[SCREEN: Browser opens to shipyard-client-portal.com/signup]

NARRATOR: You fill out the intake form. Tell the AI what you want.

[SCREEN: Form fields populate quickly — "Build me a portfolio site", "Photography focus", "Clean, minimal design"]

NARRATOR: Hit submit.

[SCREEN: Redirects to Stripe checkout, payment processes, redirects to dashboard]

NARRATOR: And now — this is the part that changes everything —

[SCREEN: Dashboard loads. Status badge: "IN PROGRESS". Progress bar animating.]

NARRATOR: You see it building.

[SCREEN: Status timeline shows: Payment Confirmed → Build Started → Installing Dependencies]

NARRATOR: Real-time. The AI isn't a black box anymore. It's a teammate with a pulse.

[SCREEN: Status updates: "Generating pages..." → "Deploying to staging..."]

NARRATOR: Twenty minutes later —

[SCREEN: Status badge changes to "READY FOR REVIEW". Notification banner slides in.]

NARRATOR: Your site is live.

[SCREEN: Clicks "View Staging Site" — New tab opens to a sleek, minimal photography portfolio]

NARRATOR: You review it. Request changes through the portal.

[SCREEN: Returns to dashboard. Clicks "Request Changes" button. Modal opens: "Make the gallery grid tighter"]

NARRATOR: The AI updates it. You see the new version.

[SCREEN: Status updates again: "Processing update..." → "Update deployed"]

NARRATOR: Approve it.

[SCREEN: Clicks "Approve & Go Live" button]

NARRATOR: It goes live.

[SCREEN: New status badge: "LIVE". Confetti animation. Two URLs displayed: Staging + Production]

---

NARRATOR: But here's the real magic —

[SCREEN: Dashboard navigation to "Retainer" tab]

NARRATOR: A month later, you need a new feature. A contact form.

[SCREEN: Retainer dashboard shows: "500,000 tokens remaining this month"]

NARRATOR: No new contracts. No new payments. Just submit the request.

[SCREEN: Types "Add contact form with validation" into request field. Clicks Submit.]

NARRATOR: The AI handles it. Instantly.

[SCREEN: Status updates in real-time: "Update in progress..." Token count decreases: 500,000 → 487,320]

NARRATOR: You see exactly how much budget you used.

[SCREEN: Update completes. Status: "Deployed to staging". Preview button appears.]

NARRATOR: Preview it. Approve it. Done.

[SCREEN: Clicks preview — contact form is live on staging. Clicks "Deploy to Production"]

---

NARRATOR: That's Shipyard.

[SCREEN: Dashboard zooms out to show full interface — project timeline, retainer balance, site preview cards]

NARRATOR: You're not waiting in the dark anymore.

You're not sending emails into the void.

You're not wondering if the AI is broken.

[SCREEN: Multiple project cards appear — "Portfolio Site: LIVE", "Blog Redesign: IN PROGRESS", "Landing Page: READY FOR REVIEW"]

NARRATOR: You're in control.

The AI builds. You watch. You approve. It ships.

[SCREEN: Final frame — Shipyard logo with tagline]

**Shipyard: Mission Control for AI-Built Websites**

[END]
